Alerts and recalls for drugs and medical devices

The following alert has been published:

Class 3 Drug Alert (Action within 5 days): GlaxoSmithKline / Parallel Distributors - Avandia 4mg, Avandia 8mg, Avandamet 1mg/500mg, Avandamet 2mg/500mg, Avandamet 2mg/1000mg, Avandamet 4mg/1000mg - EL(10)A/22

https://www.preview.alphagov.co.uk/drug-device-alerts/class-3-drug-alert-action-within-5-days-glaxosmithkline-parallel-distributors-avandia-4mg-avandia-8mg-avandamet-1mg-500mg-avandamet-2mg-500mg-avandamet-2mg-1000mg-avandamet-4mg-1000mg-el-10-a-22

Following the European Medicines Agency (EMA) recommendation on 23 September 2010 to suspend marketing authorisations for all products containing rosiglitazone across Europe, GlaxoSmithKline (GSK) announced that all presentations of Avandia and Avandamet would no longer be available after 21 October 2010.

All undispensed UK packs purchased through their approved suppliers, Alliance Healthcare and AAH Pharmaceuticals, are being recalled.

Enquiries about the content of this email should be sent to: email.support@mhra.gsi.gov.uk.
